# Hi, I'm Sai 👋

Math + CS @ UIUC. I build systems for **quantitative trading and research** — market data pipelines, signal generation, backtesting, and risk-aware execution.

Most of what I work on sits at the intersection of markets and software: pulling live data off exchanges, turning it into signals, and testing whether those signals actually hold up.

---

### 🔧 Selected Projects

**[Hyperliquid Portfolio Tracker](https://github.com/SaiPatel2006/Hyperliquid-Portfolio-Tracker)** — Real-time portfolio and market tracker for the Hyperliquid exchange. Streams live prices over WebSocket, computes account risk metrics (net equity, leverage, margin usage, unrealized PnL), and renders open positions and spot balances. Layered architecture separating data, domain, and presentation. *Dart / Flutter, Riverpod, WebSockets.*

**[Agentic Prediction Market Trader](https://github.com/SaiPatel2006/agentic-ai-prediction-market)** — Research-and-execution framework for prediction markets (Kalshi, Polymarket). Normalizes market, news, and model signals into a common schema, backtests strategy ideas (cross-market divergence, news-repricing lag, resolution mispricing, mean reversion), and routes trades through risk checks and position sizing before any execution. *Python.*

**[IMC Prosperity](https://github.com/SaiPatel2006/IMC-Prosperity-4)** — Algorithmic trading strategies built for IMC's Prosperity competition: market making, arbitrage, and signal-based positioning under simulated market conditions. *Python.*
